What is Computer Science?


Computer Science is a milieu of topics that can include anything from mobile app
development to Internet of Things (IoT) integration.

As a field, Computer Science has been monumentally influential in crafting the world as we know it today:

computer scientists have shaped the web, developed everyday tools, connected communities across the globe, and helped pioneer our adventures in the galaxy, simply to name a few.

So what is a Computer Scientist?


If packaged anything like Tazo tea (seriously read a box, especially the chai varieties), this would be my best description of a computer scientist:


Infused with ferocious, fiber optic creativity
And blended with a chorus of curiosity
that extends beyond the realm of the Milky Way,
Magic, code, and passion tango
On the fingertips of the computer scientist
To give rise to tomorrow’s solutions


A computer scientist is simply a modern-day problem solver, usually equipped with a toolbelt of technology and programming. Computer scientists can be anyone from a top-tier software developer at Google to your hometown’s art teacher: the title does not discriminate and encompasses a wide variety of individuals from a handful of backgrounds!
